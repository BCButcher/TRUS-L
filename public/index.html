How we can help...

Our service is an online marketplace for users wanting transparency from the agents competing to help them buy and sell real estate. Property owners will post basic information about their property, such as zip / postal code and approximate property value. The two million agents in North America will eagerly sign up and accept the terms of our referral agreement. Then, agents will respond to the postings with their detailed service offering and their proposed compensation. Likewise, real estate buyers will post basic details about the property that they are looking for, and again, agents will provide bids on their service offering, including the amount of money that they will pay to the buyer to use them. There are no upfront costs to agents, as the payments happen at the closing of the transaction. Agents that want to be selected will value being at the top of the first page of results that the user sees, thereby opening another revenue stream from self-promotion.
