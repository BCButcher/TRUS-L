ABOUT

TRUSÆL is the company that is reinventing the business of how 60% of the world's wealth is traded, representing $20 trillion of real estate transactions every year. TRUSÆL combines technology, real estate and marketing into one innovative company. TRUSÆL uses the existing infrastructure of a dying industry that is ripe for an overhaul, to revolutionize and modernize the real estate business. We have created an online marketplace where real estate agents compete against each other by bidding to represent buyers and sellers in transactions. Since we have are one of the first business models of this kind, there will be little competition and TRUSÆL will have the opportunity for substantial market share. If the company is able to maintain similar market share to Uber as we grow, TRUSÆL will have annual revenues of $42B CAD just from trading real estate, plus additional revenue from any paid services offered. That's great, fantastic... and we're all about being the best and making money but, not at the unfair expense of our clients.

TRUSÆL is creating a business that brings agents to clients, and clients to agents.


Our goal for the client is to connect them with a high-calibre agent providing service excellence at and a cost-effective transaction!
Our goal for the agent is to connect them with clients who fit the criteria they're offering and help build their sales portfolio!

So, how are we going to do this? Simple, TRANSPARENCY! What you can't see can hurt you and we want to take the worry and concern out of every real estate transaction by providing complete transparency. No hidden fees, no added expenses, and no surprises at closing! There are great agents looking for great clients and great clients looking for great agents... Let TRUSÆL bring you together!
